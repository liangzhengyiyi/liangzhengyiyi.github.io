Zhengyi Liang

As one of the Class of 2020, I graduated from Nanjing University in China. During my master’s program, I worked in the Computational Communication Lab with research interests in political communication and computational social science. While embracing computational methodology, I am also familiar with traditional research methods, such as experiments and surveys.

My recent project enlightens the political nature of ideology and the cultural diversity underlying political differences by analyzing large-scale data from an online reading community.
